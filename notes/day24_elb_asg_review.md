## Key Takeaways

- **Application Load Balancer (ALB)** operates at Layer 7 and supports intelligent routing features such as path-based and host-based routing. It is the best choice for modern web applications and microservices.

- **Network Load Balancer (NLB)** operates at Layer 4 and is designed for extremely high performance and low latency. It is ideal for workloads that require handling millions of connections per second and provides a static IP address.

- **Elastic Load Balancing** improves high availability by distributing traffic across healthy instances and automatically shifting load away from unhealthy ones.

- **Auto Scaling Groups** provide scalability by automatically launching or terminating EC2 instances based on demand. This helps maintain performance during traffic spikes while controlling costs during low-demand periods.
